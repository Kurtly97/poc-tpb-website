About TPB

Our Mandate

The Tourism Promotions Board (TPB) is the marketing and promotions arm of the Department of Tourism (DOT). Created through Executive Order No. 63 in 2010, TPB is tasked with marketing and promoting the Philippines domestically and internationally as a world-class tourism and MICE destination.

Our Vision

To position the Philippines as a premier travel destination through strategic marketing and promotion initiatives.

Our Mission

To increase tourism arrivals and revenues through effective marketing campaigns and partnerships.
